Where Art Becomes Soulful Creation

Welcome to SRISHTI, a space where timeless tradition meets personal expression. This page is dedicated to my original Kerala mural paintings, hand-drawn with intricate detail and deep-rooted cultural symbolism. Inspired by the classical art forms of Kerala, each piece is a reflection of stories, emotions, and a love for vibrant, soulful visuals. Every artwork here is made with care — from divine depictions to personalized portraits — blending ancient techniques with a touch of my own artistic journey.
